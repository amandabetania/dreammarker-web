Your Stage, Your Dream

DreamMaker is not just a production house; it's a platform where dreams come alive through the enchanting world of musical theater. We believe in the power of storytelling, the magic of music, and the transformative experience of live performances.

Our Mussical Theater

CATS
Genre Musical, Fantasy
Synopsis "Cats" is a musical based on T.S. Eliot's collection of poems, "Old Possum's Book of Practical Cats." The musical portrays the lives of cats during a special night known as the "Jellicle Ball." Each cat introduces itself and shares its unique story. The narrative reaches its climax when Old Deuteronomy, the Jellicle cat leader, selects the cat worthy of receiving a "rebirth."
Rebecca
Genre Romantic Drama, Mystery
Synopsis "Rebecca" is a musical adapted from Daphne du Maurier's classic novel. It tells the story of a young woman who marries the wealthy widower Maxim de Winter and moves to Manderley. There, she confronts the haunting shadow of Maxim's first wife, Rebecca, who died mysteriously. The musical unfolds a gripping mystery as dark secrets are revealed.
Wicked
Genre Musical, Fantasy
Synopsis "Wicked" is a fantasy tale that provides a different perspective on "The Wizard of Oz" story. It focuses on the relationship between Elphaba the Wicked Witch of the West, and Glinda the Good Witch of the North. The two form a friendship at a magic school and face conflicts in the land of Oz. "Wicked" explores friendship, acceptance, and judgment of differences.
